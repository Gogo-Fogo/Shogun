# Legal Documentation Index

> Purpose: route legal, compliance, privacy, data-collection, and user-facing policy questions without mixing them into general ops or research notes.

## Use this folder when the question is:

- whether an AI-assisted asset or workflow is legally safe to ship
- how privacy, consent, retention, or telemetry rules should work
- what store-policy or platform risk exists
- how ToS, EULA, clickwrap, or acceptance logging should be structured
- what unresolved legal or compliance follow-up still exists
